Terms and Conditions

Puerto Palabra

Integrated Library Management System with OPAC

1. General Provisions

These Terms and Conditions govern the use of Puerto Palabra, an Integrated Library Management System with an Online Public Access Catalog (OPAC). By registering, accessing, or using the system, users agree to comply with all library policies, regulations, and system guidelines stated herein.

Failure to adhere to these terms may result in suspension or termination of library privileges.

2. User Eligibility and Account Responsibility
  • 2.1 Only officially registered library members, librarians, and authorized administrators are permitted to access the system.
  • 2.2 Users who are eighteen (18) years old and above are required to provide a valid government-issued or school identification card during the account approval process for verification purposes.
  • 2.3 Users who are below eighteen (18) years old must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian during registration. The parent or guardian shall be responsible for completing the registration process and shall assume responsibility for the account and any transactions associated with it.
  • 2.4 Users are required to provide accurate and complete information during registration. Any falsification of information may result in denial or revocation of access.
  • 2.5 Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials. Any transaction made using a registered account shall be deemed the responsibility of the account holder.
3. Borrowing Policy

3.1 Borrowing Limit
Each registered user is allowed to borrow a maximum of two (2) to three (3) books at a time, subject to availability.

3.2 Loan Period and Extension
Borrowed materials must be returned on or before the assigned due date.
Users may request extension of borrowed books subject to the following conditions:

  • Extension is allowed up to two (2) times only.
  • The first extension request gives the user four (4) days, and the second extension request gives it two (2) days of extension.
  • Extension requests must be made before the due date.
  • Extension shall not be granted if the material is reserved by another user.
  • No additional extensions shall be permitted beyond the second time.

3.3 Non-Return Policy (One-Month Rule)
Failure to return borrowed materials within one (1) month after the due date shall result in administrative action, which may include:

  • One (1) week temporary suspension (ban) of borrowing privileges; or
  • Permanent termination of library account, subject to administrative review.

Reinstatement of account privileges shall only be granted upon settlement of all outstanding obligations and approval of the library administrator.

4. Fine and Damage Policy

As a public library, Puerto Palabra implements fines that are affordable, community-oriented, and focused on promoting responsibility rather than punishment, particularly for students and low-income users.

4.1 Classification of Book Damages
Damages to library materials shall be assessed according to the following categories:

  • Moderate Damage
    Examples include: Multiple torn pages, Ink or highlighter markings, Loose binding.
    Penalty: ₱150
  • Severe Damage
    Examples include: Missing pages, Heavy water damage, Completely damaged cover or binding.
    Penalty: ₱500

4.2 Late Return of Books
Borrowed books must be returned on or before the assigned due date. Failure to return the borrowed material on time shall result in an overdue fine of ₱5.00 per day, which will accumulate daily until the book is returned.

The overdue fine is intended to encourage timely return of library materials and ensure fair access for other users who may wish to borrow the same books.

If the borrowed material remains unreturned for one (1) month after the due date, the borrower’s account may be temporarily suspended or permanently terminated, subject to administrative evaluation.

4.3 PWD and Senior Citizen Discount
In accordance with social welfare considerations, Persons with Disabilities (PWD) and Senior Citizens are entitled to a twenty percent (20%) discount on all applicable fines, provided that a valid PWD ID or Senior Citizen ID is presented for verification.

4.4 Simplified System Implementation Policy
For efficient computation within the system:

  • Moderate Damage → ₱150
  • Severe Damage → ₱500
  • Overdue Fine → ₱5 per day
5. User Responsibilities

Users agree to:

  • Return borrowed materials on or before the due date;
  • Maintain library materials in good condition;
  • Settle all applicable fines and penalties;
  • Use the system solely for lawful and authorized library-related purposes;
  • Refrain from attempting unauthorized access, data manipulation, or system disruption.
6. Suspension and Termination

The library administration reserves the right to suspend or terminate user accounts due to:

  • Failure to return materials within the prescribed period;
  • Non-payment of fines;
  • Repeated violations of library policies;
  • Intentional damage or misuse of library materials;
  • Unauthorized or malicious use of the system.
7. Amendments

The library reserves the right to amend or modify these Terms and Conditions when necessary. Continued use of Puerto Palabra after such amendments constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
